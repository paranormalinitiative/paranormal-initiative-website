Research Area

EVP and ITC are treated as experimental research, not guaranteed communication.

The Paranormal Initiative has always actively researched Electronic Voice Phenomena and Instrumental Transcommunication. This work is approached carefully: sessions are documented, controls matter, and no recording or device output is presented as proof simply because it is unusual.

Research Position

EVP and ITC study can be meaningful without overstating what it proves. We use careful language, compare sessions against environmental and human factors, preserve uncertainty, and avoid claims that any system can guarantee contact.
