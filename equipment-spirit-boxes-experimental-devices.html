Spirit Boxes & Experimental Devices

Spirit boxes, ITC systems, SLS tools, REM-style devices, trigger objects, ACS, water ITC, and steam ITC belong in experimental research where results are documented, tested, and interpreted with care.

Equipment & Technology EVP / ITC Lessons

Guide

What They Attempt

Experimental devices attempt to create or observe conditions where responses, patterns, audio candidates, proximity changes, visual mappings, or environmental shifts can be documented. When results survive review and ordinary explanations have been seriously considered, they may support possible or plausible communication.

Major Cautions

Radio sweep can include broadcast fragments. REM-style devices can react to field changes and proximity. SLS tools can map furniture or geometry. Visual ITC can produce patterns from light, water, vapor, and expectation.

Research-First Use

Write the protocol before the session, capture controls, log device settings, preserve raw files, and use careful labels such as candidate, explainable, unresolved, possible, plausible, contaminated, or inconclusive.
