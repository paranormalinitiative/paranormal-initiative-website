Learning Program

The Paranormal Initiative Investigation Development Series is built for thoughtful growth.

The Education Center will host lessons, articles, Ghostology 101 material, field exercises, research explainers, and investigator training. The aim is to help people learn the field without sensationalism or false certainty.

Investigation Development Series

Progressive training for intake, baselines, equipment use, observation, review, and reporting.

Ghostology 101

Foundational terms, field history, theories, ethics, folklore, and responsible skepticism.

EVP / ITC Lessons

Audio experiments, controls, ACS concepts, contamination risks, and careful review language.

Field Articles

Short educational pieces on haunted locations, equipment, environmental factors, and investigation habits.

Method Exercises

Practice activities for documentation, debunking, controlled observation, and final case summaries.

Repository Pathways

Guided reading paths through the knowledge base for new and experienced investigators.
