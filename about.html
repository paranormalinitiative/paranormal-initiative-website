Welcome

The Paranormal Initiative is a community dedicated to the advancement of paranormal research, investigation, education, and exploration.

With more than 30 years of experience in the field, our mission is to encourage thoughtful discussion, responsible investigation, ongoing research, and a deeper understanding of the unexplained through critical thinking, open-minded inquiry, and evidence-based evaluation.

A Place for Investigators, Researchers, Explorers, and the Curious

We recognize that people enter this field for many different reasons. Some are investigators. Some are researchers. Some are explorers. Some are fascinated by haunted locations, local legends, folklore, and the mysteries that surround them. Others are simply curious about the unknown.

All are welcome here.

Guided by Curiosity, Not Ego

Growth occurs when we are willing to question our assumptions, challenge our conclusions, and remain open to new information. It is both honorable and credible to discover that something we once believed was incorrect after examining it from multiple perspectives.

The pursuit of truth should never be limited by ego. It should be guided by curiosity.

Active Research

EVP, ITC, Environment, Methodology, and Anomalous Experience

Our work extends beyond traditional paranormal investigation. We actively research EVP, Instrumental Transcommunication, environmental influences, investigative methodology, historical claims, and emerging theories related to consciousness and anomalous experiences.

ACS Research

ACS, the Aetheric Communication System, was created as an EVP and ITC experimentation platform designed around phonemes and allophones rather than predefined words, phrases, or language databases.

Exploration & Community

Whether you are documenting a residential case, exploring a historic location, studying folklore, experimenting with EVP, or simply learning about the field, there is a place for you here.

Core Values

Education, Research, Ethics, Truth, Honor, and Integrity remain the foundation of everything we do. If those values resonate with you, then you belong here.
