Investigation Science

Focused on the practice, planning, and methodology of paranormal investigations.

This Area Includes

  • Paranormal research methods
  • Responsible investigation techniques
  • Field preparation and investigation planning
  • Investigator development and mentorship
  • Field methodology and best practices
  • Residential, business, historical, and public-location investigations
  • Team organization and investigative workflows
