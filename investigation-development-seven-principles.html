The 7 Principles of The Paranormal Initiative

Paranormal investigation should be more than chasing activity or reinforcing beliefs. It should be built on responsibility, documentation, and a commitment to following the evidence wherever it leads.

Series Index Standards & Ethics

Raising The Standard

The Paranormal Initiative was created with a simple goal: help raise the standards of paranormal investigation.

That means treating field work as a careful process of observation, documentation, analysis, client care, and responsible communication. These are the principles that guide that mission.

1. Follow The Evidence, Not The Belief

Investigators must allow evidence to guide conclusions rather than allowing beliefs to shape interpretations.

2. Document Everything

Events, environmental conditions, equipment readings, and investigator observations should always be carefully documented so investigations can be reviewed accurately.

3. Rule Out Natural Causes First

Environmental factors, structural conditions, weather, electrical fields, and other natural explanations must always be considered before labeling activity as paranormal.

4. Respect The People Asking For Help

Investigators often enter homes where people are frightened or confused. The goal should always be to provide understanding and reassurance, not fear.

5. Approach Every Case With An Open Mind

Responsible investigators remain open to both natural explanations and unexplained possibilities.

6. Investigation Before Assumption

An investigation should always be a careful process of observation, documentation, and analysis, not immediate conclusions.

7. Professionalism Strengthens The Field

The credibility of paranormal research depends on investigators who approach cases with honesty, integrity, and responsible investigative methods.

Follow The Evidence

The Paranormal Initiative supports investigators who believe the paranormal field deserves better tools, better documentation, and better investigative standards.

By working together as a community of investigators, researchers, and explorers of the unknown, we can help bring greater credibility and professionalism to the field.

Follow the evidence, not the belief.
