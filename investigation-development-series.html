Education Center Investigation Methodology

Series Focus

Case Preparation

Intake, claims review, location context, witness care, access planning, safety notes, and team expectations before field work begins.

Field Discipline

Baseline observation, logging habits, team roles, contamination control, equipment notes, and calm documentation during an investigation.

Review & Reporting

Evidence handling, natural explanations, uncertainty, client communication, and responsible final summaries.
