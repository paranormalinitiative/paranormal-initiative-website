Historical & Cultural Research

Understanding the history, culture, folklore, and documented context surrounding locations and reported phenomena.

  • Historical documentation
  • Property and land history
  • Folklore research
  • Archival studies
  • Newspaper and public-record research
  • Cultural interpretations of unusual experiences
  • Preservation of local stories and historical accounts
