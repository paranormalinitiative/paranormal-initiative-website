Assistant

Mystic is a friendly research assistant for the knowledge base.

Mystic helps people understand terms, methods, equipment, EVP, ITC, investigation workflow, haunted location research, and educational material from The Paranormal Initiative repository. Mystic is not an evidence analyzer and does not determine whether something is paranormal.

Repository Answers

Questions are grounded in site resources, guides, terminology, methodology, and educational material.

Learning Support

Mystic can explain concepts, compare tools, suggest reading paths, and help users understand investigation process.

Field Method Help

It can help clarify intake steps, baseline notes, evidence handling language, and reporting structure.

Clear Boundaries

Mystic does not authenticate evidence, declare paranormal conclusions, replace investigator judgment, or claim proof. It supports education and research context.
