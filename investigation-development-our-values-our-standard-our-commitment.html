Our Foundation

At The Paranormal Initiative - Applied Paranormal Research and Studies, our foundation is built on five core values.

Education. Research. Ethics. Truth. Honor and integrity.

These are not just words to us. They are the principles that guide everything we do.

A Stronger Field Begins With Respect

We believe the paranormal field grows stronger when people are willing to learn, ask questions, share knowledge, and challenge ideas respectfully.

This community is built on mutual respect.

There is no room here for bullying, hatred, personal attacks, belittling others, unnecessary drama, or tearing people down because they think differently.

Everyone Starts Somewhere

Everyone starts somewhere.

Some members of our community have decades of investigative experience. Others are picking up an EMF meter or researching their first case.

Every one of those people deserves to be treated with respect.

Do Not Guess. Learn.

If you do not know the answer to something, do not be afraid to say so.

Do not guess. Do not make something up.

Ask questions. Research. Listen. Learn.

There is absolutely nothing wrong with saying, "I do not know, but let us find out together."

In fact, that is often where the best learning begins.

A Place To Learn Together

We are building a place where curiosity is encouraged, evidence is discussed respectfully, and knowledge is shared freely.

It should be a place where experienced investigators can mentor those just beginning, and where newcomers feel comfortable asking questions without fear of being ridiculed.

Through our investigative team, the Somerset Paranormal Research Society, we strive to put these values into practice in the field.

Educational Resources For Every Level

Through The Paranormal Initiative - Applied Paranormal Research and Studies, we are committed to creating educational resources that help investigators of every experience level continue to grow.

Whether you have investigated for thirty years or thirty minutes, whether you are a researcher, historian, weekend explorer, investigator, or simply someone curious about the unexplained, you are welcome here.

Raising The Standard Together

Let us learn together.

Let us research together.

Let us challenge ideas respectfully.

Let us raise the standard of paranormal investigation one conversation, one investigation, and one discovery at a time.

Lifelong Learning

Education. Research. Ethics. Truth. Honor and integrity.

Responsible investigation begins with curiosity, earns credibility through discipline, and continues through lifelong learning.
