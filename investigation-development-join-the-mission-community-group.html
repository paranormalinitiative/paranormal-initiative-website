The Paranormal Initiative - Applied Paranormal Research and Studies is built around education, research, ethics, truth, honor, integrity, and a shared desire to raise the standard of responsible paranormal investigation.

A Place For Curious Minds

Whether you are an investigator, researcher, historian, weekend explorer, equipment user, EVP/ITC experimenter, or simply curious about the unexplained, there is room for you in the conversation.

The goal is not to tell people what to believe. The goal is to learn, investigate, respect, document, understand, and keep asking better questions together.

Respectful Discussion Matters

We want this community to be a place where people can ask questions, share experiences, challenge ideas respectfully, and learn from one another.

That means keeping the focus on curiosity, evidence, education, and responsible conversation rather than drama or personal attacks.
