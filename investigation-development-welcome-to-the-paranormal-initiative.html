Who We Are

The Paranormal Initiative is a community dedicated to the advancement of paranormal research, investigation, education, and exploration.

With more than 30 years of experience in the field, our mission is to encourage thoughtful discussion, responsible investigation, ongoing research, and a deeper understanding of the unexplained through critical thinking, open-minded inquiry, and a commitment to evidence-based evaluation.

Everyone Enters Differently

We recognize that people enter this field for many different reasons.

Some are investigators. Some are researchers. Some are explorers.

Some are fascinated by haunted locations, local legends, folklore, and the mysteries that surround them. Others are simply curious about the unknown.

All are welcome here.

Growth Requires Honest Questions

At The Paranormal Initiative, we believe growth occurs when we are willing to question our assumptions, challenge our conclusions, and remain open to new information.

We believe it is both honorable and credible to discover that something we once believed was incorrect after examining it from multiple perspectives.

The pursuit of truth should never be limited by ego. It should be guided by curiosity.

Research Beyond Traditional Investigation

Our work extends beyond traditional paranormal investigation.

We actively research EVP, Electronic Voice Phenomena, ITC, Instrumental Transcommunication, environmental influences, investigative methodology, historical claims, and emerging theories related to consciousness and anomalous experiences.

This commitment to research led to the development of ACS, the Aetheric Communication System.

Why ACS Exists

ACS was created as an EVP and ITC experimentation platform designed around phonemes and allophones rather than predefined words, phrases, or language databases.

The goal is not to prove communication, but to provide a cleaner environment for experimentation and the continued study of anomalous voice phenomena.

Research Is Only Part Of The Journey

Research is only one part of the journey.

We also recognize the importance of exploration, community, and shared experience.

Whether you are documenting a residential case, exploring a historic location, searching for local legends, kayaking to forgotten places, studying folklore, experimenting with EVP, or simply learning about the field, there is a place for you here.

Tools, Resources, Education, And Community

The Paranormal Initiative exists to provide tools, resources, education, discussion, and community for anyone interested in exploring the unknown.

Our core values remain the foundation of everything we do: Education, Research, Ethics, Truth, Honor, and Integrity.

If those values resonate with you, then you belong here.
