Education Center · Area of Study

Artificial Intelligence

Understanding how artificial intelligence can support paranormal research, education, documentation, review, and communication without replacing investigator judgment or responsible evidence evaluation.

This Area Includes

  • AI literacy for investigators and researchers
  • Responsible AI use in educational writing, editing, and organization
  • Source verification, citation habits, and research transparency
  • AI-assisted evidence review boundaries and human oversight
  • AI-generated graphics, labeling, disclosure, and public trust
  • Hallucinations, bias, false confidence, and interpretation risks
  • Digital integrity, metadata awareness, and preservation of original files
  • Ethical safeguards for client information, witness testimony, and case data
  • Future AI-assisted tools for education, documentation, and research support
