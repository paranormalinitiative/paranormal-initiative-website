Florida

20 Locations

Florida contains one of the broadest paranormal location profiles in the southeastern United States, with major concentrations in St. Augustine, Tampa/Ybor, coastal military forts, historic hotels, and burial grounds. The state is especially valuable for comparative investigation because it combines colonial, military, Gilded Age hospitality, maritime, and folklore-driven environments across both urban and coastal settings.
