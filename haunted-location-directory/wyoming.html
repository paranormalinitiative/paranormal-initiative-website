Wyoming

18 Locations

Wyoming combines prisons, frontier forts, railroad-era hotels, ghost-town landscapes, civic buildings, churches, libraries, and national-park lodges into one of the West’s most diverse historic-haunting reference states. This build prioritizes public-facing sites with strong structural identity, repeatable claim traditions, and usable investigation zones across Laramie, Rawlins, South Pass City, Fort Laramie, Thermopolis, Buffalo, Cheyenne, Fort Bridger, Casper, Yellowstone, Cody, Hartville, Sheridan, and Green River.
