Wisconsin

12 Locations

Wisconsin combines haunted hotels, vice-era bars, prison properties, campus landmarks, civic buildings, museum ships, fire-disaster memorial sites, and spiritualist landmarks into one of the Midwest’s most varied historic-haunting reference states. This build prioritizes strong public-facing locations with usable structure zones, repeated claim traditions, and investigation-ready context across Milwaukee, Baraboo, Fond du Lac, Madison, Kenosha, Maribel, Manitowoc, Peshtigo, Waupun, and Whitewater.
