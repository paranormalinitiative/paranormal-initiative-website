Georgia

15 Locations

Georgia is one of the strongest paranormal-reference states in the South, anchored by Savannah’s dense historic-core cluster and reinforced by Civil War battlefields, coastal resort properties, antebellum homes, and major urban cemeteries. The state offers unusual depth for comparing hospitality, burial-ground, battlefield, and domestic cases within a single regional file.

The Marshall House

Hotel Haunted Historic
Savannah, GA
Historic Hotel
Access: Public / Guest Access

Opened in 1851 and later used as a Civil War hospital, The Marshall House is one of Savannah’s strongest hospital-overlay hotel cases. Reports of wartime residue and guest-room disturbances have accumulated across decades of modern hotel use.

Activity Reported

  • Voice anomalies in quiet upper floors
  • Reports of medical-era apparitional imagery

Colonial Park Cemetery

Cemetery Haunted Historic
