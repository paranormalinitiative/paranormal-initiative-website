West Virginia

11 Locations

West Virginia’s Haunted Location Directory focuses on real, identifiable haunted destinations that can support guided exploration and evidence logging. These entries use rooms, zones, or outdoor buckets as user-facing evidence containers rather than strict investigation floorplans, allowing visitors to organize photos, audio, notes, and flags by meaningful areas.
