Equipment Study Standard

From The Paranormal Initiative Education Series, the first rule of field technology is simple: equipment measures physical conditions. It does not identify spirits, demons, hauntings, or proof. A reading only becomes useful when it is tied to time, location, settings, baseline, operator notes, and possible ordinary causes.

Start With The Reading

A number, light, sound file, photo, or video clip is a recorded observation. It should be logged before it is interpreted.

Add The Context

Useful equipment notes include time, room, device settings, operator position, baseline readings, weather, and nearby ordinary sources.

Review Before Claiming

Most unusual readings need follow-up. Check repeatability, contamination, nearby devices, building conditions, and human movement first.
