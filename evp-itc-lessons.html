EVP / ITC Study Workflow

01

Define the Session

Write the question, method, device settings, environment, participants, and rejection criteria before capture begins.

02

Capture Baselines

Record room tone, device behavior, ordinary reflections, water or steam movement, and known contamination sources before looking for candidates.

03

Preserve Originals

Keep raw audio, video, image, and session files intact. Review copies can be enhanced, but originals remain the reference record.

04

Review Without Leading

Use independent review where possible. Avoid telling reviewers what phrase, face, shape, or response you expect them to perceive.

05

Classify Modestly

Use careful categories: explainable, contaminated, unresolved, candidate, inconclusive, or further review needed.
